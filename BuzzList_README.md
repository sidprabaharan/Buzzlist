
# **BuzzList: Online Platform for Kingston Bars** 🍹  
A streamlined app for Queen’s University students and the Kingston community to simplify bar-hopping by providing live wait times, a virtual queuing system, and group queue management for local bars.

---

## **Overview**  
BuzzList enhances the going-out experience by helping users find the perfect bar based on:
- **Live wait times**
- **Opening hours**
- **Special events**
- **Type of music**
- **Drink prices**

Users can join a bar's queue virtually, eliminating the need to wait outside. The app generates a QR code for seamless entry once they reach the front of the queue. Bar staff can scan QR codes to check users in quickly, reducing stress during busy hours.

---

## **Features** 🚀  
### For Bar Attendees:
- **Search and Filter:** Discover bars based on current wait times, drink prices, and more.
- **Virtual Queue:** Join the waitlist online for yourself or with a group.
- **Group Queueing:** Start a group and invite friends to join.
- **Notifications:** Receive real-time updates on queue position and wait times.
- **QR Code Entry:** Skip the line and show your QR code at the door.

### For Bar Staff:
- **QR Code Scanning:** Efficiently check in attendees at the door.
- **Blacklist Management:** Restrict access for users violating bar policies.

---

## **Target Audience**  
1. **Bar Attendees:**  
   - Students and locals seeking to skip long lines and enjoy a seamless bar-hopping experience.  
2. **Bar Staff:**  
   - Managers and bouncers looking to streamline entry and reduce the stress of large crowds.

---

## **Success Stories** ✨  
- **Jack (Student):** Jack and his friends skip the cold, track their queue position in real-time, and receive QR codes to enter bars quickly.
- **Becca (Bouncer):** Becca checks in attendees efficiently using QR codes, avoiding heated interactions in long lines.  
- **Kevin (Local):** Kevin and his out-of-town friends filter bars based on preferences and join the waitlist before leaving home.
